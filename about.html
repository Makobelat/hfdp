ABOUT HAVEN

Haven for Disabled People is a Sharpeville-based non-governmental organization devoted to creating a nurturing, supportive, and empowering environment for individuals with disabilities. Founded with a vision of inclusion and dignity for all, Haven serves as a refuge for those facing physical, intellectual, or developmental challenges, offering a comprehensive range of services designed to enhance independence and personal growth.

At the heart of Haven’s mission is a commitment to breaking down societal barriers and advocating for equal opportunities. The organization offers holistic programs, including vocational training, skill-building workshops, accessible education, and recreational activities, all aimed at improving quality of life and facilitating community integration.

Haven for Disabled People has become a critical resource in Sharpeville, forming partnerships with local businesses, healthcare providers, and government agencies to expand accessibility and foster a community where people of all abilities can thrive. Beyond providing immediate assistance, Haven engages in community outreach and advocacy, working to shape policies and raise awareness about the needs and rights of people with disabilities.

Through continuous fundraising efforts, community events, and volunteer involvement, Haven for Disabled People remains a pivotal force in Sharpeville, inspiring change, hope, and inclusion for one of society’s most vulnerable communities.

About us

Haven Haven for Disabled People, located in the heart of Sharpeville, is a non-governmental organization dedicated to empowering and uplifting individuals with disabilities. Since its inception, Haven has been a beacon of hope and support for the disabled community, providing essential resources, inclusive programs, and advocacy to foster independence and improve quality of life. With a focus on education, vocational training, and social integration, Haven for Disabled People actively works to break down societal barriers and create a more accessible and inclusive environment.
